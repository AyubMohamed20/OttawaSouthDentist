## Key Differentiators

### Direct Billing
Family Dental Care offers direct billing to insurance companies, eliminating the need for patients to pay upfront and wait for reimbursement. The practice handles all paperwork and billing directly with insurance providers.

### Emergency Dental Care
The practice provides emergency dental services for urgent dental issues including:
- Severe toothaches
- Broken or chipped teeth
- Lost fillings or crowns
- Dental injuries

### CDCP Acceptance
Family Dental Care accepts the Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP), making dental care more accessible to eligible Canadians.

### Patient-Centered Approach
- Focus on stress-free dental experiences
- Patient education is a priority
- Comprehensive treatment explanations before procedures
- Comfortable environment for anxious patients

### Comprehensive Services
The practice offers a full range of dental services under one roof:
- General dentistry
- Preventive care
- Cosmetic dentistry
- Restorative treatments
- Orthodontics (Invisalign)
- Oral surgery
- Dental implants

## Practice Philosophy

Family Dental Care believes in:
- Educating patients about their oral health
- Providing personalized treatment plans
- Using modern dental technology
- Creating a comfortable, welcoming environment
- Building long-term relationships with patients and families
