## Direct Billing to Insurance

Family Dental Care offers **direct billing** to most insurance companies, making dental care more accessible and hassle-free.

### How Direct Billing Works

1. **Provide your insurance information** – Give us your insurance details at your appointment
2. **We verify coverage** – Our team confirms your benefits with your insurance provider
3. **Treatment is performed** – You receive your dental care
4. **We bill directly** – We submit the claim directly to your insurance company
5. **Pay only your portion** – You only pay any remaining balance not covered by insurance

### Benefits of Direct Billing

- **No upfront payment** – You don't have to pay the full amount and wait for reimbursement
- **Less paperwork** – We handle the claim submission for you
- **Know your costs** – We can estimate your out-of-pocket costs before treatment
- **Convenient** – Makes dental care more accessible

### Insurance Providers

We work with most major insurance providers in Canada. Please bring your insurance card to your appointment, and our team will verify your coverage.

## Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP)

Family Dental Care is proud to accept the **Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP)**, making dental care more accessible to eligible Canadians.

### What is CDCP?

The Canadian Dental Care Plan is a federal government program designed to help Canadians who don't have access to dental insurance. The program provides coverage for various dental services.

### Eligibility

To be eligible for CDCP, you generally must:
- Be a Canadian resident
- Have an adjusted family net income below the threshold
- Not have access to dental insurance

### Services Covered

The CDCP covers a range of dental services including:
- Preventive care (cleanings, checkups)
- Diagnostic services (X-rays, exams)
- Restorative treatments (fillings)
- Endodontic services (root canals)
- Periodontal services (gum treatments)
- Prosthodontic services (dentures)
- Oral surgery services

### How to Use CDCP at Our Office

1. Apply for the Canadian Dental Care Plan through the federal government
2. Once approved, bring your CDCP documentation to your appointment
3. We will bill the program directly for covered services
4. You may be responsible for any services not covered by the plan

## Insurance FAQs

**Do you accept my insurance?**
We work with most major insurance providers. Contact our office with your insurance information, and we'll verify your coverage.

**What if my insurance doesn't cover the full cost?**
You will be responsible for any portion not covered by your insurance. We can provide an estimate before treatment so you know what to expect.

**Can you help me understand my insurance benefits?**
Yes! Our team can help explain your coverage and estimate your out-of-pocket costs for recommended treatments.

**What if I don't have insurance?**
We welcome patients without insurance. We offer various payment options and can discuss treatment plans that fit your budget.

**Do you offer payment plans?**
Please contact our office to discuss payment options for larger treatments.
